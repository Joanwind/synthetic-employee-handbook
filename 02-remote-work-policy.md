# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility
Remote work is available to employees whose roles can be effectively performed outside the office, subject to manager approval. Eligibility is determined based on job responsibilities, performance, and business needs. Employees may be approved for full-time remote or hybrid arrangements depending on team requirements. The company reserves the right to review and adjust remote work arrangements as operational needs evolve.

## Working Hours
Employees are expected to work their contracted hours and remain available during core business hours, typically 9:30am to 4:30pm Irish time. Flexibility outside these hours may be agreed with line managers, provided business needs are met. Employees should clearly communicate their working schedule and notify their manager of any changes. Regular attendance at scheduled meetings and timely task completion are expected.

## Communication
Remote employees must maintain consistent and proactive communication with their teams using approved tools such as email, messaging platforms, and video conferencing. Employees are expected to respond to messages within a reasonable timeframe during working hours. Participation in team meetings, check-ins, and collaborative sessions is required. Clear and professional communication is essential to ensure alignment and productivity.

## Equipment
The company will provide essential equipment such as laptops and necessary software to support remote work. Employees are responsible for maintaining company equipment in good condition and using it primarily for work-related purposes. A reliable internet connection and a suitable workspace are required for effective remote working. Any technical issues or equipment faults should be reported promptly to IT support.

## Data Security
Employees must adhere to all company data protection and cybersecurity policies while working remotely. Confidential information should only be accessed and stored on approved, secure systems and devices. Use of public or unsecured Wi-Fi networks is prohibited unless a company-approved VPN is in place. Employees are responsible for safeguarding company data and reporting any suspected security incidents immediately.
