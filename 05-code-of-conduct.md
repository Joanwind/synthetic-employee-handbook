# Code of Conduct

## Professional Behaviour
Employees are expected to conduct themselves in a professional, ethical, and responsible manner at all times. This includes meeting performance expectations, adhering to company policies, and maintaining appropriate standards of communication and conduct in both in-person and remote settings. Employees must act in the best interests of the company and avoid behaviour that could harm its reputation. Misconduct, including dishonesty, negligence, or inappropriate use of company resources, may result in disciplinary action.

## Respect and Inclusion
The company is committed to providing a workplace that is inclusive, respectful, and free from discrimination. Employees must treat colleagues, clients, and partners with dignity and fairness, regardless of background, identity, or personal characteristics. Discriminatory behaviour, exclusion, or biased decision-making will not be tolerated. All employees are expected to contribute to a positive working environment where diverse perspectives are valued.

## Harassment Policy
Harassment of any kind, including verbal, physical, visual, or online conduct that creates an intimidating, hostile, or offensive environment, is strictly prohibited. This includes bullying, inappropriate comments, unwanted advances, and any behaviour that undermines an individual’s dignity. Employees are encouraged to report any incidents of harassment promptly through designated reporting channels. All complaints will be taken seriously, investigated confidentially, and addressed in line with company procedures.

## Conflict of Interest
Employees must avoid situations where personal interests conflict, or could reasonably be perceived to conflict, with the interests of the company. Any potential or actual conflicts must be disclosed to management promptly. This includes external employment, financial interests, or relationships that may influence decision-making. Employees must not use their position for personal gain or engage in activities that compromise their objectivity or integrity.
