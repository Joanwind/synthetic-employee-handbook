# Benefits and Compensation

## Salary Structure
The company offers competitive salaries aligned with market benchmarks in Ireland and reviewed regularly to ensure fairness and consistency. Salary levels are determined based on role, experience, skills, and individual performance. Employees are paid monthly via electronic transfer, and salary reviews are typically conducted annually as part of the performance review process. Any adjustments are subject to business performance and individual contribution.

## Bonuses and Incentives
Employees may be eligible for discretionary performance-based bonuses, depending on individual, team, and company performance. Bonus schemes are reviewed annually and are not guaranteed unless explicitly stated in the employment contract. In certain roles, additional incentives such as project-based rewards or retention bonuses may apply. The company may also offer recognition programmes to reward exceptional contributions.

## Health Benefits
The company provides access to private health insurance schemes to support employee wellbeing. Coverage options may vary by role and tenure, with the possibility to include dependants at the employee’s cost. Employees also have access to an Employee Assistance Programme (EAP), offering confidential support for personal and work-related issues. Health and wellbeing initiatives may be introduced throughout the year.

## Pension Scheme
Employees are eligible to join the company pension scheme in line with Irish regulations. The company offers employer contributions subject to minimum employee contributions and scheme rules. Details of contribution levels and eligibility are provided upon enrolment. The scheme is designed to support long-term financial planning and retirement savings.

## Additional Benefits
The company offers a range of additional benefits to support employee wellbeing and development. These may include flexible working arrangements, wellness initiatives, and access to learning and development resources such as training courses and certifications. Employees may also benefit from paid leave policies, social events, and recognition programmes. Benefits are reviewed periodically and may evolve to meet employee needs and business priorities.
